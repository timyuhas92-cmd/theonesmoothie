The One Smoothie Recipe

  • 12–16 oz unsweetened almond or Fairlife milk
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 tsp spirulina powder
  • 1 tsp beet powder
  • 1 scoop supergreens powder
  • 1 scoop collagen
  • ½ cup blueberries
  • ½ cup strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen spinach

Blend everything with ice to your desired consistency and enjoy! Adjust liquid for a 20–24 oz serving.
